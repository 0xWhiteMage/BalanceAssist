## 5. Services offered (full list)

The studio offers seven service pillars plus deep sub-service expertise:

1. **Production** — cinematic shoots, branded films, corporate videos. "On set, every detail matters: from lighting and camera movement to sound capture, set design, and performance direction."
2. **Post-Production** — the studio's deepest specialty. Editorial & video editing, 3D animation, 2D/frame-by-frame animation, high-fidelity 3D modeling, VFX & compositing. Tool stack named: **Blender, DaVinci Resolve, Premiere Pro, After Effects**. Process in four stages — Rough Cut, Online Edit, Refinement & Enhancement, Quality Control & Final Delivery.
3. **Event & Experience Content** — large-scale projections, anamorphic displays, AR/VR, interactive installations, stage content. Studio capability line: "We don't just support events — we redefine them into unforgettable experiences."
4. **Media Asset Adaptations** — film cutdowns, social snippets, event visuals, multilingual/local versions. "Your story is not just resized — it's reimagined to thrive across every medium."
5. **Design & Direction** — storyboards, key visuals, art direction. "We craft atmospheres, moods, and experiences that captivate audiences and leave a lasting impression."
6. **Generative AI** — hybrid workflow where AI accelerates but does not replace artistic craft. Capabilities: design/concept, immersive set design, motion control, expensive VFX shots (fire, smoke, debris, collisions), visual style exploration, face swap, AI voice-over with perfect lip-sync, background transformation. Featured project: F1 Racing (Hybrid AI Work).
7. **Title Design** — motion typography, opening titles. "Bold entry points — capturing audiences, shaping perception, and amplifying impact across digital, cinematic, and live experiences."

Plus **Creative Campaign** services (strategy, insight-driven creative, full campaign systems) and **Animatic** pre-visualization.

## 6. Process and methodology (their own framing)

For Production:
- **Pre-production** — concept, storyboards, technical recce
- **On set** — direction, lighting, performance, sound capture
- **Post** — editorial, color, audio, finishing
- **Quality Control & Final Delivery** — "rigorous quality checks… optimized for distribution across all required channels, from broadcast to social media campaigns."

For Post-Production specifically, four named stages:
1. **The Rough Cut Stage** — "We often get the Client 2 round of Feedbacks to maximize the expectations."
2. **Online Editing Stage**
3. **Refinement & Enhancement** — color grading, audio mixing
4. **Quality Control & Final Delivery**

Differentiators: **Full Process Control** (every stage in-house), **Proven Expertise** (10+ years, top-tier clients), **Seamless Workflow** (one-team integration), **Attention to Detail**.

## 15. FAQ (their own framing)

- **What is the difference between post-production and film post-production?** — Same thing in modern terms. Balance applies cinematic-level detail (editing, color, audio, VFX) regardless of source format.
- **Why a production company over a freelancer?** — Eliminates skill gaps; one accountable team; cohesive quality.
- **How are video production costs calculated?** — Three factors: pre-production scope, filming-day scale, post-production complexity.
- **How long does a typical project take?** — 6 to 12 weeks.
- **How do I choose the right production house?** — Strong portfolio, clear process, proven in-house expertise, glowing testimonials.
- **What services do you offer?** — End-to-end: Strategy & Concept, full-service Production & Filming, comprehensive Post-Production, including editing, animation, VFX, sound design, plus Live Events and Immersive Experiences.

## 17. What Balance is not for

- Single-task work (a freelancer may be cheaper for that).
- Pure strategy consulting without creative execution.
- Non-video deliverables (e.g., print, branding-only) — Balance is video/visual-first.
- Anything that requires lying about results — the studio's brand is built on candor and craft.
