## Tagline

## In one paragraph

**Balance Studio** is a Singapore-based, full-service video and creative production house. Founded 10+ years ago, the studio is trusted by 100+ global clients and partners, has delivered 110+ projects worldwide, and operates from Singapore with a Vietnam team. The studio's thesis: "Balance represents the perfect stage of Design Innovation & Creative Evolution, empowering brands with confidence to complete their missions."

## What they do — services

End-to-end creative production, with the studio's own in-house specialists on every stage:

1. **Production** — Cinematic shoots, corporate video, branded films. Scouting, direction, on-set mastery.
2. **Post-Production** — Editing, sound design, colour grading, finishing. In-house 2D/3D animation, motion graphics, VFX.
3. **Event & Experience Content** — Event visuals, stage content, AR/VR experiences, anamorphic OOH.
4. **Media Asset Adaptations** — Localization and resizing for languages, markets, platforms.
5. **Design & Direction** — Storyboards, key visuals, art direction.
6. **Generative AI** — Concept exploration, AI-driven storyboards, scalable art direction.
7. **Title Design** — Intro animations and motion identities for film/streaming/brands.

## Timeline

6–12 weeks for a typical professional video project end-to-end (pre-production + filming + post-production). Variables: pre-production planning, number of filming days, complexity of post-production (editing, animation, VFX).

## Cost model

Cost is determined by three factors: pre-production creative development, scale of filming days, and complexity of post-production.
